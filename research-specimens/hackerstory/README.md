# HackerStory research specimens

Status: `published fragmentary artifacts / research specimens`

This directory is a public research surface for three AI-mediated HackerStory
dossiers. They are published because their incompletion, repetition, unstable
classification, and generated mutation logic are part of the work.

They are not presented as finished literature, settled research, canonical
storyworld, or a transparent account of their source materials. They are
specimens of a generative editorial process caught while changing state.

Included:

- `index.html`
- `merge-hackerstory-session-8.html`
- `pool-hackerstory.html`
- `merge-hackerstory-copilot-tasks.html`

Publication frame:

- The three dossier editions are exact byte copies of the Drive renders from
  `03_product/hackerstory-html/` on 2026-06-22.
- Their publication wrappers add specimen-status language without rewriting
  the dossier bodies.
- The source DOCX files are not included.
- The collection is intentionally linked from the public front.
- Names of source nodes and generated interpretations are visible. This is an
  explicit boundary opening authorized on 2026-06-22.
- Publication does not canonize any claim, character relation, mutation,
  source classification, or generated prose passage inside the dossiers.
- The collection may be revised, contradicted, split, or withdrawn as the
  repository continues to become something else.
