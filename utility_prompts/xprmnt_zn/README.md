# XPRMNT-ZN Proposed Utilities

Status: repo-prepared / proposed / not installed.

This folder stages small XPRMNT-ZN utility prompts that are useful enough to preserve in the repo, but not installed as public-front features, default utilities, doctrine, or canonical game-layer apparatus.

These prompts may be used for internal repo play, testing, and future review when the material asks for a concrete move. They should not be promoted into public copy, service interfaces, chatbot behavior, onboarding language, or general doctrine without a separate decision.

## Current Proposed Utilities

- `GRINDODE__locked_door_odesrad.md` - compresses a blocked material-body through its actual locked door and produces an ödesrad.
- `EFTERHANDSBETT__damage_successful_form.md` - damages a form that has become too usable, proud, or merit-ready.
- `BOTFORBUDET__figure_preservation.md` - protects a figure or apparatus from becoming a service interface.

## Shared Boundary

Use these when a material-body needs one playable line, one status consequence, or one preservation move.

Do not use them for:

- general backstory
- cosmic fate
- public-front copy
- service design
- chatbot roles
- automatic installation
- doctrine promotion

If a line sounds wise, epic, or like lore, lower it back into a concrete object, stop condition, or failed route.
