A public experimental passage

A membrane that also lets selected bodies pass: research specimens, method figures, ceremonies, and playable utilities.

Start with status, not import

If something belongs here, first name its source boundary, allowed use, forbidden use, and next human decision. This page points to the method before it points to material.

Status

XPRMNT-ZN is represented here as a public membrane and an experimental passage. This page is not the Drive zone or an originals mirror, but it can carry selected public bodies whose form depends on mutation, friction, or incomplete status.

The repo can show method, public figures, ceremonies, playable utilities, and research specimens without bulk-importing every source body behind them.

What can pass

Readers can read the public surface. Critics can test the method. External collaborators can send bounded ideas through Norrotiv. What passes through the membrane is changed by the apparatus; it does not become a feature request, support claim, or ownership claim.

What does not pass

Drive originals, credentials, privata uppgifter, oklara rättigheter, dashboards, community loops och chatbot-liknande tjänstefigurer hör inte hemma på denna yta. Avgränsning innebär inte att publika kroppar måste beskrivas som väntande eller omogna.
