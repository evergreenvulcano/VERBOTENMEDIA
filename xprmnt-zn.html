XPRMNT-ZN

A public membrane, not the zone itself

A readable surface for Radio, Astrologi, Monsternotis, and the boundary rules that keep experimental material from becoming a dump, app, or service.

Status

XPRMNT-ZN is represented here as a public membrane. This page is not the Drive zone, not an originals mirror, not a contamination zone, and not a protected-material import.

The repo can show method, safe pointers, reviewed status notes, and public-ready afterimages. It must not bulk-import XPRMNT-ZN material just because the zone matters.

Agent-readable XPRMNT-ZN note → Sluice register template → Doctrine: XPRMNT-ZN as membrane →

What can pass

Readers can read the public surface. Critics can test the method. External collaborators can send bounded ideas through Norrotiv. What passes through the membrane is changed by the apparatus; it does not become a feature request, support claim, or ownership claim.

Norrotiv → Verboten Media →

What does not pass

Drive originals, raw source dumps, protected storyworld material, dashboards, community loops, chatbot-like figures, and generic product explanations do not belong on this surface. Pointer notes may help agents avoid mistakes, but they do not activate new public functions.

Praktikanten pointer note → Wise Consultant pointer note →

Time and banketter

Banketter are the ceremonial time logic for material that has become ready to render. They are not updates, progress bars, or launch notes. XPRMNT-ZN can point to bankett logic before any bankett text is promoted.
