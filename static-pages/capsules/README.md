# Capsules

Capsules are short public-facing knowledge units published by VERBOTEN MEDIA.

They are not project summaries. They are not marketing texts. They are not full essays. They are not substitutes for literary prose.

---

## What a capsule is

A capsule names something precisely — a rule, a boundary, a status, a provenance fact, a method fragment, or a pressure point. It is complete in itself without needing to be long.

Capsules may include:

- **Provenance capsules** — where a piece of work came from, how it was produced
- **Pressure maps** — notes on what a project is pressing against or working through
- **Publication diagnoses** — status assessments of a text or body of work
- **AI-status notes** — declarations about the role of AI in production
- **Method fragments** — concise statements of editorial or publication method
- **Editorial rules** — operating principles for how VERBOTEN MEDIA works

---

## What a capsule is not

A capsule does not claim to be literary prose. It does not speak in the authorial voice of a literary work. It does not replace an essay. It does not summarize a project body for marketing purposes.

---

## AI-mediated production

Capsules may be AI-mediated when the capsule does not claim to be literary prose or direct authorial voice. A capsule about AI, a status declaration, or a method rule can be produced with AI assistance while remaining honest about what it is.

Literary prose, essays, and origin-bearing authorial texts remain outside this permission.

---

## Location

All capsules live in `static-pages/capsules/`. They are rendered through `page.html?path=static-pages/capsules/{filename}.md`.
