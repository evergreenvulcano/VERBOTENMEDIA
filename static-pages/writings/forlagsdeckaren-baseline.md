## Working opening

Förläggaren arrives before the forklifts wake. The logistics floor still smells of cardboard dust and machine oil. On paper this building is transit space; in practice it has become a dwelling, an office, an archive, and a crime scene where the missing object is always the same: certainty.

The first clue appears as an editorial decision. A manuscript is accepted, then delayed, then moved, then renamed. No one lies directly. The system lies through sequence.
