This Patreon begins with three texts that explain what Verboten Media is trying to protect.

Not content.

Not a personal brand.

Not a productivity stream.

A literary project has its own pressure, memory, provenance, and social function.

These opening texts are not polished marketing explanations.

They are closer to foundation documents: attempts to say why literature still matters, why the author-function changes in the AI era, and why certain wounds, fragments, voices, and artefacts must be carried carefully rather than converted too quickly into content.
