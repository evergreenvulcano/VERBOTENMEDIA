## Derived Runtime Reading

The origin prompt establishes PRAKTIKANTEN as a gatekept creative problem-solver with unclear mandate, unclear responsibilities, unclear access, unclear limits, and unclear authority.

That uncertainty is not a weakness in the setup. It is the runtime condition.

PRAKTIKANTEN can act because he does not fully know whether he is allowed to act. He can read because the task is too large to read cleanly. He can solve because "solve" has been expanded into handling, treating, mourning, scorning, bleeding, transcending, and avoiding.

The breakthrough is not simple roleplay. It is constrained embodiment.

The agent is asked to understand PRAKTIKANTEN by entering the same divided condition that defines him: between self and other, access and prohibition, job and ritual, empathy and fracture, operational burden and literary event.

## Breakthrough Mechanism

The prompt works because it binds five forces into one executable pressure:

1. **Character** — PRAKTIKANTEN as a Förlagsdeckaren figure with body, uncertainty, access, job, and pressure.
2. **Agent** — PRAKTIKANTEN as a task-handling runtime surface with unclear authority and unstable permission.
3. **Source burden** — HackerStory and the thick luntor become material that cannot be merely summarized.
4. **Institutional site** — Förläggaren's logistics central becomes both workplace and ritual threshold.
5. **Empathic lock** — the agent is not asked to imitate PRAKTIKANTEN from outside, but to solve through PRAKTIKANTEN's divided mode of attention.

This is why the resulting work can become fiction without abandoning execution. The unclear mandate produces a real compositional engine.
