## 1. The Half-Step Slowdown

The half-step slowdown is the strongest scene-operable unit in the current field.

Earlier threshold work emphasized lingering, departure delay, return cycles, and transitional architecture. The new form is smaller and more exact:

```text
A body adjusts before the mind has an account of the adjustment.
```

Possible carriers:

- cup near the counter edge;
- drawer left open just enough to change route;
- printer lid down over completed pages;
- light already on in a space that does not require light;
- chair angled so passage remains possible but slower;
- a plate left reachable but not offered.

The cluster should be handled as behavioral micro-editing, not as symbolism. It works only if no one in the room immediately explains why the body slowed.

Mutation pressure:

The slowdown must retain ambiguity between care, habit, spatial accuracy, and governance. If the text names it as strategy, the node overheats. If it becomes atmosphere only, it dissipates.

Scene test:
