# Logistikcentral Consultation Runtime

## Status

developmental, non-canon, non-public

## Role

This runtime treats the logistikcentral as institutional, domestic, and industrial body.

Förläggaren frames the situation as manuscript consultation. Other figures resist, deform, reroute, or literalize that frame until the building's mixed ontology becomes active: publishing house, home, warehouse, nervous system, workplace, and scene machine.

## Operating Principle

The consultation is never only about the manuscript. The logistikcentral makes every question spatial, infrastructural, domestic, and institutional at once.

## Use

Use this runtime when a scene needs:

- Förläggaren converting any problem into editorial consultation
- domestic-industrial interruption
- institutional material revealed through logistics, rooms, cables, food, weather, delivery, or maintenance
- characters resisting the publisher's frame without simply defeating it
- manuscript pressure crossing into physical infrastructure

## Constraints

Do not reduce the logistikcentral to setting. It is an active body and pressure system.

Do not let Förläggaren's manuscript frame become the whole truth. The consultation should be useful and false at the same time.

## Failure Modes

- The logistikcentral becomes scenery.
- Consultation becomes literal workshop dialogue.
- Domestic material becomes softness rather than structural force.
- Industrial material becomes aesthetic decoration.
- Resistance to Förläggaren becomes simple refusal instead of deformation or rerouting.

## Review Hook

Check whether the scene makes the logistikcentral act as body, institution, and home at once.
