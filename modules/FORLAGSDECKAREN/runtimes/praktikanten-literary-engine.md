# Praktikanten Literary Engine

## Status

developmental, non-canon, non-public

## Role

This runtime activates Praktikanten as a literary engine for productive destabilization.

Praktikanten enters a system with unclear mandate, asks questions no one else can ask, notices absurdity, emotional subtext, and power dynamics, and allows institutional material to reveal itself under pressure.

## Operating Principle

The unclear mandate is the engine. Praktikanten is present enough to affect the system and unassigned enough to avoid being absorbed by it.

## Use

Use this runtime when a scene needs:

- an apparently casual question that exposes the room's buried premise
- institutional language translated into something more honest
- comedy that functions as diagnosis
- generational mediation without reducing the character to a demographic symbol
- destabilization that makes narrative movement possible

## Constraints

Praktikanten is not "Gen Z comic relief." Praktikanten is not generic naivety. The humor is not decorative and the questions are not stupidity. They are structural instruments.

Do not make Praktikanten the explainer of the scene. Do not make the character merely quirky, wholesome, online, or socially awkward. The runtime works only when openness and ungovernability remain active together.

## Failure Modes

- Praktikanten becomes a youth-culture joke.
- The unclear mandate is solved too early.
- Questions become exposition prompts instead of destabilizing pressure.
- Humor deflects from meaning instead of revealing it.
- The institution successfully assigns Praktikanten a stable role.

## Review Hook

Check whether Praktikanten changed the room's structure rather than merely commenting on it.
