# Semantic Orientation

Status: canonical doctrine for current public-front build work.
Readiness: provisional.

Note: This doctrine is accepted as a working northstar, but it may need to be rebuilt
when the difference is clearer between repo-internal rebuild rhetoric and the repo's
final collaborator-facing usefulness.

## Core Terms

### Public Front

A public boundary surface for a work. Not a website, product launch, portfolio, or
onboarding system. It means the work can be read externally without being turned
into a service.

Decision rule: design for filtering and exposure, not for conversion.

### Public Passage Apparatus

A structure that lets work become visible without absorbing the reader into user,
community, or product roles.

Decision rule: make passage possible without promising access, support, or belonging.

### Membrane

A boundary surface where some things can pass and others cannot. What passes is
changed by passing.

Decision rule: ideas may be tested, washed, absorbed, or rejected.

### XPRMNT-ZN

The experimental apparatus: Radio + Astrologi + Monsternotis. It is the public
membrane for the system.

Decision rule: XPRMNT-ZN is not a showcase, tool, app, or finished product.

### Norrotiv

The collaborator/contact path where external actors may test ideas or send
criticism. It is not a community channel, support queue, or feature-request system.

Current contact, if needed: `verbotenmedia@protonmail.com`.

Decision rule: Norrotiv may receive ideas, but it does not grant ownership,
response guarantees, or governance.

### Bankett

A ceremonial time marker. A Bankett gives a work period status, closure, and a
prospective opening. It is not a blog post, changelog, progress report, or news item.

Decision rule: Banketter mark ceremonial time instead of measuring progress.

### Witness

Someone who reads or follows the work without demanding something from it.

Decision rule: write for witnesses, not users.

### Collaborator

An external actor who tests ideas against the apparatus. A collaborator can affect
the work without owning or steering it.

Decision rule: collaboration is bounded by the apparatus.

### Public-Bound

Ready for public publication. It carries enough context to stand without hidden
assumptions or protected source exposure.

### Public-Safe

Can be public only with surrounding context. It must not stand alone.

### Storyworld-Protected

Source-protected material that must not be published: Del 1, `aphorism_source`,
origin material, BYN/LEGION/SORL, Praktikanten philosophy, and adjacent protected
source anchors.

Decision rule: never publish this material or small excerpts from it for context.

### Preparation Residue

Preparatory files used to design the system. They can inform work but are not canon
by default.

Decision rule: archive or leave residue where it is unless a task explicitly
promotes a distilled result.

### Canonical Doctrine

Files that permanently or provisionally govern the repo because they define its
principles.

Decision rule: doctrine is required reading for public-front work.

| Public front | Website/portfolio | Filter and expose without selling |

| XPRMNT-ZN | Project showcase | Center the experimental apparatus |
| Norrotiv | Community/support | Receive tests without granting control |
| Bankett | Changelog/blog | Mark ceremonial time |
| Witness | User/audience | Read without service expectation |
| Collaborator | Co-owner | Test without steering |
| Public-bound | Public-safe | Can stand alone |
| Public-safe | Public-bound | Needs context |
| Storyworld-protected | Merely private | Never public |
| Preparation residue | Canon | Do not auto-promote |
