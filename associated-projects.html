Linked Initiatives
and Infrastructure

Projects connected to the VERBOTEN MEDIA field — independent but part of the same extended inquiry.

VERBOTEN MEDIA maintains active connections with a set of linked initiatives that share its concerns, methods, or origin. These projects operate independently, but they occupy common intellectual and infrastructural ground. This section documents those connections.

LÄNK Active

Critical Digital Culture Platform

LÄNK is a platform for critical writing on digital culture, technology, and their social and aesthetic consequences. It operates as a publication and a curatorial project — selecting, commissioning, and presenting texts that take contemporary digital life seriously as a subject for analysis. LÄNK shares VERBOTEN MEDIA's commitment to intellectual independence and editorial precision, while occupying a distinct position as an explicitly digital-critical outlet.

NORROTIV Active

Narrative and Cultural Research Initiative

NORROTIV is the unfinished interaction surface for northern narrative gravity, idea tests, collaborator pressure, and bounded repo proposals. It is not Verboten Media and not a generic research consultancy. It lets external actors bring a thought near the work without having that thought automatically become imprint canon.

Förlagsdeckaren Published

The Publishing Detective — Literary Work

The inaugural literary project of VERBOTEN MEDIA. A hybrid work — documentary, literary, analytical — that examines the structures, pathologies, and unexamined assumptions of independent literary publishing through the figure of the detective narrative. The project was the infrastructural origin of the platform: built because no existing structure was adequate to hold it. Available through VERBOTEN MEDIA directly.
