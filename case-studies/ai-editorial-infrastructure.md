# AI Editorial Infrastructure

## What this shows

The repository contains workflows for moving from text mass to staged candidates, normalized markdown, public pages, and operational memory. AI is used as an editorial and structural collaborator, not as a blind content mill.

## Why it matters

Large creative corpora need routing, provenance, review states, and publication thresholds. This repo shows that method in working form: source, staging, proposal, public content, and operational memory remain distinct.

## Public-facing value

The work demonstrates an editorial system that can preserve voice while making AI-assisted publication auditable, repeatable, and less chaotic.

## Current status

Operational and evolving. The markdown publishing path is the clearest public-facing part; the `_gpt` and app surfaces remain machine-room evidence.
