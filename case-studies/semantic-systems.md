# Semantic Systems

## What this shows

VERBOTENMEDIA treats meaning as infrastructure. The repo includes semantic runtime modules, source anchors, glossary controls, admission gates, and method documents that shape how text and systems are allowed to transform.

## Why it matters

AI-assisted work becomes useful when language, provenance, and authority are handled explicitly. The semantic systems here prevent source material from becoming a shapeless prompt pile.

This case shows system design around text: how concepts, constraints, and runtime boundaries can be made inspectable enough for serious AI-assisted creative work.
