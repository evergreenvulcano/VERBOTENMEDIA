# Syscall contract: `activate_underhallningsmode`

Status: v0.1 static entertainment contract  
Date: 2026-06-10

## Purpose

Maintain `underhallningsmode.html` as external text-as-interface where a visitor can approach Förlagsdeckaren as a skönlitterär arbetskropp without being forced into “content”, “portfolio”, “CTA”, or “chatbot” logic.

## What it may do

- create static routes such as “I do not want to read”, “Give me a Förläggare”, “Show me the machine room without leaking it”, “I have an idea”, and “I want to get lost”
- link to public-ready writings, capsules, runtext, skalle, XPRMNT-ZN afterimages, and contact/Norrotiv routes
- produce short public-ready fragments when explicitly authorized
- clarify that underhållning is not content, skönlitteratur is not content, and fiction can carry entertainment
- stage the Förläggare as working for the reader even when the reader is not reading

## What it must not do

- implement a chatbot
- pretend to personalize a visitor's experience
- import raw/source/protected material for flavour
- turn Förlagsdeckaren into generic content marketing
- reduce Underhållningsmode to a gimmick or conversion path
- collapse Norrotiv idea intake into Verboten Media canon

## Input

- target visitor mood or route
- allowed public pages
- optional public-ready fragment list
- explicit publication status

## Allowed reading

- public site pages
- public-ready writings and capsules
- XPRMNT-ZN representational notes
- `_rebuild` contracts and operator briefs

## Forbidden reading

- protected/private material
- Drive originals
- contamination zones
- raw source dumps

## Allowed writing

- `underhallningsmode.html`
- navigation/footer links
- small public-ready static fragments if explicitly authorized
- `_rebuild/` notes documenting activation decisions

## Output

A static page or static fragment. No runtime personalization. No chatbot. No hidden prompt layer. The generative force stays in the förlagets world and text machine, not in a personalized content feed.

## Stop conditions

Stop if the proposed route requires private source access, pretends to speak as a live Förläggare, or makes entertainment indistinguishable from generic engagement mechanics.
