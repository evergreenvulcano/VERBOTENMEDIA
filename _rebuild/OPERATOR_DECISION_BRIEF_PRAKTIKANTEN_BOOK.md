# Operator Decision Brief: Praktikanten Book

Status: implementation brief / public book body / no prose rewrite.
Date: 2026-06-27.

## What changed

`Praktikanten` now has a dedicated public book hub:

- `praktikanten-boken.html`
- `static-pages/praktikanten-book/`

The hub presents the book as a single literary body and routes readers into the chapter files through the existing `page.html` markdown reader.

## What was published

- 21 chapter and apparatus markdown files.
- A public table of contents and reading order.
- A strong route from `praktikanten.html`.
- One discrete Writings registry entry.

## What was not changed

- The Drive source was not modified.
- The creative prose was not semantically rewritten.
- The book was not split into 23 Writings cards.
- Praktikanten was not turned into a bot, guide, interface, service, mascot or onboarding surface.

## Text treatment

The public chapter copies received only two mechanical treatments:

- technical Drive/work headers were removed;
- em dash punctuation was replaced with ordinary hyphen punctuation.

Diegetic AI, LLM, prompt and machine passages remain as source prose.

## Recommended next decision

Decide whether `praktikanten-boken.html` should become a top-level nav item across every public page or remain surfaced primarily through `praktikanten.html`, `writings.html` and the landing page entrances.
