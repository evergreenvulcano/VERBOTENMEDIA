What it is

VERBOTEN MEDIA is the publishing body: an imprint, a narrative infrastructure, and a literary entertainment apparatus built to hold work that does not behave like ordinary content. It can publish texts, stage public entrances, preserve status, and make the machine room legible without dumping it on the reader.

It is not a creative studio, an AI lab, a SaaS idea, or a productivity system. It is a literary and publishing world with operational surfaces.

What belongs here

Förlagsdeckaren belongs here as skönlitterär arbetskropp and entertainment, not as content marketing. The Förläggare, the publishing apparatus, the public logs, and the comic seriousness of a machine that keeps working even when nobody reads it are Verboten Media material.

Skalle / second-brain imagery may appear here as diegetic object, prop, skull, lodging, or joke. It must not become the repo's self-description.

What does not belong here

Norrotiv's contributor traffic should not be mistaken for direct entry into the imprint. Martin's personal authorship should not be flattened into platform copy. Machine-room notes are public-readable when they are in GitHub, but they are not automatically public-ready.
